We are delighted to announce a new partnership that has been two years in the making. Starting this month, Northwind Traders is the exclusive importer for the **Hochtal Alpine Cheese Cooperative** — a group of eleven family dairies working the high summer pastures above the Hochtal valley.

If you have ever tasted a true mountain cheese, you know why we chased this one. The cows graze meadows full of wild herbs and alpine flowers all summer, and you can taste every one of them in the finished wheel.

## Who they are

The cooperative was formed in the 1970s so that small dairies could share a single ageing cellar without losing their independence. Each farm still makes its own cheese by hand, in copper vats, over a wood fire — but they age the wheels together in a cellar cut into the mountainside, turning and brushing each one by hand for up to eighteen months.

The result is a range with real range:

- **Hochtal Sommer**, a young, supple wheel aged four months, sweet and grassy.
- **Hochtal Reserve**, aged twelve months, with the crystalline crunch and brothy depth you want in a mountain cheese.
- **Hochtal Winter**, made from richer autumn milk and aged the longest, for the cheese counters that want something truly special.

## What it means for your counter

Because we are importing direct from the cooperative, there is no broker in the middle. That means fresher wheels, full traceability back to the individual farm, and pricing that gets a fair share back to the dairies.

The first chilled shipment lands next week and will be on our seasonal list. We expect demand to outrun supply for the first few months, so if you want a wheel of the Reserve for your counter, get your order in early.

We could not be prouder to bring these cheeses to you. They are exactly the kind of food Northwind exists to find.
